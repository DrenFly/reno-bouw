WHO WE ARE & OUR PRINCIPLES

Founded back in 2007 as a family business, Reno-Bouw-Rajczyk is now a full-service company with a fast growing presence on the Dutch market. Having successfully completed over 300 projects since then, working closely with designers and architects to deliver high quality services with a personalised approach, we are confident in our ability to convert your ideas in to a finished product. Our diversified experience and the extensive partnerships we have established is what ensures that we exceed the client’s expectations.

Review our gallery to see that we have completed many projects and transformed many living spaces into modern, comfortable ambient homes that left many satisfied clients to enjoy their new feel.
